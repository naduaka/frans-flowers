Meet Fran

Fran has been arranging flowers since she was twelve years old, learning the craft from her grandmother in a small garden in the countryside. What started as a childhood hobby became a lifelong passion.

In 1998, she opened Fran's Flowers with nothing but a borrowed van, a folding table, and a belief that everyone deserves beautiful flowers. Over two decades later, the shop has become a beloved neighborhood staple.

Every bouquet is still arranged by hand with the same care that went into the very first one. Fran believes flowers aren't just decoration — they're how people say the things words can't.
